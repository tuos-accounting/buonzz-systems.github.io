What "tuos" means?

May 22, 2019

Darwin Biler

This project is initially just called "Biler Accounting", due to lack of initial idea how to call it. I decided to assign it some project name, to easily distinguish it with other brands and leave that "X factor", the same way that quickbooks or SAP is almost equivalent to the word "accounting system".

After searching for cool startup name in namelix, I really don't like the bunch of things it generated. So I resorted of thinking Filipino words that is not purchased yet (domain and github repo).

Tuos basically means account in english, and the term Accounting directly translates into pagtutuos.

Why Filipino word? - it is simple gesture to honor my country and somewhat show to the community that Philippines can be also a place where great software startup could rise up.
