Business Pain Points

Tuos Accounting is specifically built to solve problems that plagues Digital Marketing Agencies such as:

Proper Inventory of Domains and Servers across Hosting Providers

Once you are managing more than 10 domains, spread across GoDaddy, Namecheap, AWS Route 53, it is very easy to get confused which domain is hosted to which. Pair that with hosting provider like Google Cloud Platform, Digital Ocean and so on. The complexity increases as you now have to consolidate which domain is pointing to which provider.

Quickbooks and other leading accounting software is "too general"

QBO and other CRM software caters to lot of industries - manufacturing, tourism, brick and mortar stores etc. Tuos, is laser-focused on catering to companies who publishes and manage websites, social media accounts and anything in-between.

IT Infrastructure Expenditure monitoring and Forecasting

Cloud-based hosting like AWS, Azure and GCP is not cheap. Having the ability to monitor these expenses and see if these costs "justifies" the profit that you were making out of your sites is a very valuable tool in running your digital agencies.

Streamlined Team workflow for Launching Sites

If you are having trouble of keeping tab of "Go-to Live Checklist" (e.g. installing GA codes, configuring name domains etc.) when launching new website, you'll like our product. We have built-in task/projects section that is specially designed for launching new websites.
