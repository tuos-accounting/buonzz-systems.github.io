Business Pain Points

Tuos accounting software is built to solve problems that occurs today. Most of the software you see in the market today was built 10-20 years ago, which means they are built to solve problems that exists in the past. As each vendor attempts to "patch" the old software to cope up with the ever-changing business requirements, it results into bloated platform.

Tuos however started in a clean slate. Not being bugged down by tons of legacy software. It is built specifically to solve problems pragmatically that really exist now, rather than obeying fixed theories, ideas, or rules of legacy codebases.

Here is a list of business pain points that Tuos attempts to solve:

Vendor Lock-in

Huge Upfront costs

Expensive Software Licensing

Customizations is very hard/expensive

Lack of agile

In-house team

Too many dependencies

Expensive tech support

Business is too small
