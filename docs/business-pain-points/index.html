Business Pain Points

Tuos Accounting is specifically built to solve problems that businesses currently have. If you are having any of all of the following pain points, then Tuos might be a perfect fit for you.

Vendor Lock-in

Most of the accounting system tends to get you into a vendor "lock-in", wherein they are making you dependent for products and services, unable to use another vendor without substantial switching costs. For example, SAP-related products tends to use SAP HANA (a proprietary database), that only SAP develops.

Tuos uses open-source technologies which is very popular among IT professionals. That means you can easily get people to customize or maintain our system in case you don't want to avail of our customization services.

For example, we are using MySQL database, a very popular database. And there is a huge pool of System Administrator, Database Administrator and IT professionals that is well versed in administering this database, thus you wont have any problem maintaining and customizing it for your needs without being locked to us.

Low success rate when developing in-house solutions

Developing in-house solution customized for your business process is never easy. You need to invest huge amount in training, planning and hiring numerous consultant and project managers to ensure the success of the project.

According to this study, only 34% of projects finish on time and only 42% come in on budget.

Tuos is developed with the mindset that it can be customized for specific use-case of your business. This saves you and your team a huge amount of time creating the foundation for your accounting system by simply building on top of our system.

A lot of modules is pre-built, so that it addresses common mundane processes. For example Audit Trail, Role-based permission, Invoice management etc. This allows your in-house developers to focus on business logic that is unique your business, instead of re-creating everything from the scratch.

Expensive Software Licensing

Try comparing Linux (free) vs Windows (Paid) and then MySQL CE (free) vs SQL Server (paid). There is very little difference in terms of feature that your business actually need. Most of the licensing cost associated with paid software are things you dont actually need:

for example:

  • Windows Server user interface is more user-friendly - do you really need your server to be user-friendly? I bet most of the time, the people who will login to your server are IT expert and professionals (who knows what they are doing). Besides, the Windows UI takes enormous amount of memory and CPU resource -- resource that could had been used by your business software instead.
  • SAP HANA - this database is so expensive because it allows you to do the following: "predictive analytics, spatial data processing, text analytics, text search, streaming analytics, graph data processing" --- hmm, but ask yourself, do you really need all of those? I bet if you only want to record your cash receipts transactions and generate financial reports yearly, most probably you dont don't need any of those.

Tuos uses open-source, yet industry proven technologies that has lower Cost of Ownership, both for short and long term initiatives.

  • CentOS - is a Linux server software that is widely used for most highly trafficked websites. Whenever you are browsing a website in the Internet, most likely it is running in Linux server.

  • NodeJS - is a highly scalable and fast runtime environment for developing web applications. It is being used by large companies to run their IT infrastructure, few of the examples are Netflix, PayPal, Uber, Walmart, eBay, NASA, Yahoo and Twitter, source: Top Companies that Used Node.js in Production

  • MySQL - is a very fast, multi-threaded, multi-user, and robust SQL (Structured Query Language) database server. This software is a battle-hardended database server used in most mission critical applications. Here are few companies that uses MySQL: YouTube, Airbnb, Nokia, JP Morgan Chase & Co, Tesla etc, source: mysql.com/customers

All of the above technologies doesn't require you to purchase licenses for you to use in production. If you are worried about support, it is very easy to avail of commercial support either.

Modern problems requires modern solution

Tuos accounting software is built to solve problems that occurs today. Most of the existing software you see in the market was built 10-20 years ago, which means they are built to solve problems that exists in the past. As each vendor attempts to "patch" the old software to cope up with the ever-changing business requirements, it results into bloated platform.

Bloated platforms, combined with restraints of backward compatibility support for old installations makes the software really expensive to maintain. Unfortunately, these costs is being passed on to consumers (like you). In reality, a huge percentage of money you pay for most vendors is spent on maintaining THEIR platform, rather than features that actually solves YOUR problems.

Tuos however started in a clean slate. Not being bugged down by tons of legacy software. It is built specifically to solve problems pragmatically that really exist now, rather than obeying fixed theories, ideas, or rules of legacy codebases.
