Features

Open-Source

Low overhead cost

You dont need to spend a lot of money for server licenses. We are using MySQL and NodeJS which means the server license is free.

Professional Support

Rest assured, you will always have someone to help you in case you are having issues.

Features

Products and Service Management

Boost your sales by organizing your Offers and Promos efficiently. This allows you to understand the customer buying pattern and maximize your profit based on this information. With the Customer Management capabilities paired with Rewards and Upsell feature, it easily allows you to increase the Customer Lifetime Value

Invoicing and Cash Receipts

Issue and Receive Invoice to easily track Income and Expenses.You can easily generate Cash Receipts Book to track all your cash receipts. This allows you seamlessly keep on track of the most important metrics of your business - Cash!

Cash Disbursement and Purchasing System

Need a way to track the business expenditure? Our Cash Disbursement reports can easily give you an understanding where your money goes. Paired with Purchasing System, your Employees can make a Purchase Order via the system, so that the Purchasing Department can easily canvas and make the purchase -- each step of the process can be tracked by both side (Requestor and Purchasing Officer).

Who is Using This?

This product is aimed for Small and Medium-sized enterprise who wants a quick, simple and straight-forward solution to manage their business instead of engaging with complex and expensive ERP Systems such as SAP Business One, IBM Maximo or Salesforce.
