Features

Consolidate a list of your Domains and Web Properties

Easily view a list of all domains you own, what nameserver it is pointing to, its SSL certificate status and so on. Having the ability to view all of these in one place makes it easier to manage your digital properties. Tuos also keeps track of revenue and expenses associated with those sites so you have a better understanding which domains is the most important ones.

IT infrastructure expenditure Reporting and Forecasting

Hosting, Domain and Storage costs is not cheap. You need to make sure you are accounting these properly and avoid unnecessary expenditures.Knowing when to scale up and scale out base on traffic is good. But you should also consider whether those additional expenses is justifiable by the profit that those site is bringing into the table

Team Skills and Capacity Planning

Your team might be composed of people who have varying skills and expertise. Some might know design, backend coding or DevOps functions.
In able to launch new websites efficiently, you need to ensure you have the right combination of people for that particular project. Tuos allows you to align skills needed by a Project with the skills available in your team.
